SUPPORTED MATHEMATICAL FUNCTIONS:

1. Arithmetic Operations:
    Addition: +
    Subtraction: -
    Multiplication: *
    Division: /
    Exponentiation: **

2. Trigonometric Functions:
    Sine: sin(x)
    Cosine: cos(x)
    Tangent: tan(x)
    Arcsine: asin(x)
    Arccosine: acos(x)
    Arctangent: atan(x)

3. Exponential and Logarithmic Functions:
    Exponent: exp(x)
    Base-10 Logarithm: log10(x)
    Natural Logarithm: ln(x)

4. Hyperbolic Functions:
    Hyperbolic Sine: sinh(x)
    Hyperbolic Cosine: cosh(x)
    Hyperbolic Tangent: tanh(x)
    Arctangent: asinh(x)
    Arccosh: acosh(x)
    Arctanh: atanh(x)

SUPPORTED CONSTANS:
1. PI (pi: 3.14159)
2. Base of thenatural logarithm (E: 2.71828)
3. Golden ratio (phi: 1.61803)
4. Euler’s constant (euler: 0.577216)
5. Catalan’s constant (catalan: 0.915966)
